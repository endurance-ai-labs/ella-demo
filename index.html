Questions, answered

Everything people ask before they call.

Which channels does Remi capture?

All of them: inbound and outbound phone calls, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet video meetings, and in-person conversations captured on any device. Every channel flows into one memory per customer.

How does Remi's memory work?

Every conversation is transcribed and filed to the right customer automatically. The next time that customer calls — or shows up on a video meeting, or walks in — Remi recalls every prior detail, name, and promise and picks up exactly where you left off.

Does Remi just record, or does it act?

It acts. Remi nurtures leads, books and confirms appointments, sends contracts, collects payments, requests reviews, and moves ready buyers across the finish line — based on what it hears.

Do we need HighLevel to use Remi?

No. Remi works fully stand-alone. If you already run HighLevel, we'll build Remi natively into your existing sub-account at no extra cost.

How long does setup take?

Days, not months. Onboarding is included free on Yearly and Lifetime plans, and every plan includes free customizations so Remi sounds like your business from day one.

Can callers still reach a human?

Always. Press 0 at any point and Remi transfers the call to your team — with the full conversation context attached, so nobody repeats themselves.
